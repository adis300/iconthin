WORLD CLASS PRODUCTION FACILITY



We produce 100% natural astaxanthin from Heamatococcus pluvialis with the highest quality standards throughout a holistic production line. Microalgae biomass is cultivated in closed tubular photobioreactors with highly purified water and CO2 to guarantee a contamination-free environment. We are dedicated to producing the world’s highest quality astaxanthin for human consumption with our cutting-edge technologies and rich experience in microalgae products.
